Dividend Strategy

Dividend investing focuses on companies that pay consistent and growing dividends. It is suitable for investors seeking regular passive income while benefiting from potential capital appreciation. This strategy is commonly used in retirement planning, as dividend stocks provide stable cash flow. High-dividend-paying companies are typically well-established, low-volatility stocks in sectors like utilities, consumer goods, and finance. However, dividend yields should be balanced with growth potential to avoid investing in companies with unsustainable payouts.
